# SMS Terms and Conditions

## Program Description

This SMS program provides account, service, support, and operational notifications to users who opt in.

## Opt In

Users opt in by texting START, YES, or SUBSCRIBE to the business phone number.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies and is expected to be low volume.

## Costs

Message and data rates may apply according to the user's mobile carrier plan.

## Opt Out

Users may cancel SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. After opting out, the user may receive one final confirmation message.

## Help

Users may reply HELP for assistance.

## Eligibility

Users must be the owner or authorized user of the mobile number used to opt in.

## Changes

These terms may be updated as business messaging practices or compliance requirements change.
