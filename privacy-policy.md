# Privacy Policy

## Overview

This privacy policy explains how contact information and SMS messaging data are handled.

## Information Collected

We may collect a phone number, message opt-in status, message history, and support-related information provided by the user.

## Use of Information

Information is used to send requested SMS notifications, provide support, confirm opt-in status, process opt-out requests, and maintain compliance records.

## Sharing

SMS opt-in data and consent are not sold or shared with third parties for their marketing purposes.

## Opt Out

Users may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP.

## Help

Users may request support by replying HELP.

## Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are used to protect contact and messaging data.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the business administrator using the published support channel.
